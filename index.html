You're in control

Thermetrik is designed for people who are passionate about cooking, so you can customise everything. Use the probe for sous vide, deep frying, slow cooking, caramelizing sugar or tempering chocolate. Use the integrated temperature sensor for pan frying and searing.

Redefined recipes

Try new dishes with the integrated recipe book. Create your own recipes and Thermetrik will follow the exact temperature settings and let you know when to add ingredients. Use the built in tried and tested recipes to discover new amazing food at restaurant quality.

Demo

We have made a fully functional prototype. It is lots of fun to use and we can't wait to share it with the world! Watch us use it to melt tempered chocolate without losing the temper. This is extremely difficult to do without a Thermetrik - the temperature of the chocolate has to get to 33 °C but must stay below 34 °C - a tight range!
